Viscosity Calculator (Dynamic & Kinematic Fluid Mechanics Tool)

This free viscosity calculator helps you calculate dynamic viscosity, shear stress, and kinematic viscosity using standard fluid mechanics formulas. It is useful for mechanical engineering, chemical engineering, and fluid analysis.

Formulas Used

Dynamic Viscosity:

μ = τ / γ

Shear Stress:

τ = μ × γ

Example Calculation

If shear stress (τ) = 50 Pa and shear rate (γ) = 10 s⁻¹, then:

μ = 50 / 10 = 5 Pa·s

Kinematic Viscosity:

ν = μ / ρ
